# Senior DevOps Engineer

## About the Role
We are looking for a Senior DevOps Engineer to design, build, and operate the
infrastructure and delivery pipelines that power our cloud-native platform. You
will own reliability, automation, and developer experience across our AWS
environment, working closely with backend and platform teams.

## Responsibilities
- Design and manage Kubernetes (EKS) clusters, including autoscaling, networking, and workload isolation
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines for containerized microservices
- Own Infrastructure as Code using Terraform across multiple AWS accounts
- Implement observability: metrics, logging, tracing, and alerting (Prometheus, Grafana, CloudWatch)
- Drive reliability through SLOs, incident response, and post-incident reviews
- Improve security posture: IAM, secrets management, least-privilege access, IRSA
- Optimize cloud cost and performance
- Mentor engineers on DevOps best practices and automation

## Required Qualifications
- 5+ years in DevOps, SRE, or Platform Engineering roles
- Strong hands-on experience with AWS (EKS, EC2, VPC, IAM, S3, RDS)
- Production experience operating Kubernetes at scale
- Expertise in Terraform (or equivalent IaC) and GitOps workflows
- Proficiency with Docker and container build/security tooling
- Strong scripting skills in Python, Bash, or Go
- Experience with CI/CD systems (GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, ArgoCD, or Jenkins)
- Solid understanding of Linux, networking, and distributed systems

## Nice to Have
- KEDA / event-driven autoscaling experience
- Service mesh (Istio, Linkerd) and ingress controllers (AWS Load Balancer Controller)
- Experience with messaging/streaming (SQS, Kafka, Kinesis)
- Certifications: CKA/CKAD, AWS Solutions Architect / DevOps Engineer

## What We Offer
- Ownership of a modern, cloud-native platform
- Strong engineering culture with automation-first mindset
- Remote-friendly, flexible work environment
